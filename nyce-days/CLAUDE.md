# Nyce Days

Marketing site for Nyce Days (nycedays.com). Next.js 14 App Router + TypeScript,
Tailwind + shadcn/ui, Supabase, framer-motion. Deploys to Vercel on push to `main`.

## Writing copy

Any visitor-facing copy (pages, components, metadata, email, SMS) must follow the brand
voice. Read it first:

@BRAND_VOICE.md

Quick rules: no em dashes, no emoji. Title Case buttons and headlines, lowercase nav and
eyebrow labels. The signature is "Have a nyce day." Never invent product types, stats,
dates, or place names.
